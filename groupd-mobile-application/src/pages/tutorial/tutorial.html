Welcome to Groupd

The social networking app that helps you find creative projects, and like-minded individuals to work on projects with.

How does Groupd work?

Groupd allows users to post projects, add members to projects, have customisable profiles with a list of projects they may have created or been a member of, and explore other projects and profiles.

Looking for team mates to work on a project idea you have?

Simply search users by name or their skills!

Looking for a project to work on?

Just search projects by name or their tags! Or explore the home page for the latest projects!
